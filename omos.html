About Green-Tech

Green-Tech is an online platform where various tech companies can find inspiration and assistance for green and sustainable solutions for their production. Whether it involves hardware components, computer cases, microchips for their motherboards, mobile technology, etc., there is an opportunity to find new partners or be inspired to come up with ideas on how to become more sustainable, and thereby also help protect the environment.

Green-Tech was founded in 2024 by a danish student named Daniel Toft.

Our Mission

Our mission at Green-Tech is to reach out to as many tech companies as possible and help to focus on green transition and sustainability in the tech industry. We not only strive to raise awareness but also to assist companies in finding sustainable ways to produce their components. For example, by using sustainable and recyclable aluminum to produce cooler fins for CPU coolers, using sustainable copper for the small components in computer hardware, using sustainable ABS plastic for hardware plates, etc. There are plenty of opportunities to embrace sustainable and greener solutions.
